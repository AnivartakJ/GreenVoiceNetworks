What We Do

We foster impactful change through in-depth research and strategic insights to ensure sustainability changes.

We specialize in studying successful and failed case studies of green entrepreneurs, finally

equipping SMEs with actionable strategies derived from our findings.

High-quality Research


Our Mission : We aim to set the benchmark for high-quality research through our case study analysis of green startups. Our method involves a thorough examination of both successful and unsuccessful ventures,offering a detailed look into the strategies and challenges faced by these businesses. By gathering extensive data through founder interviews, financial performance evaluations, and market assessments, we uncover valuable insights and trends. This approach enables us to identify best practices and common pitfalls, providing actionable recommendations tailored to the unique needs of green entrepreneurs. Our commitment is to deliver evidence-based strategies that foster sustainable growth and innovation in the green business sector.

About me


Anivartak Jain (Founder @GVN) My journey began at the Vellore Institute of Technology, where I graduated in 2024 with a degree in Computer Science. While my academic background is in technology, my passion has always been rooted in green entrepreneurship. I was captivated by the potential of businesses to make a positive impact on the environment and society. I often pondered why successful businesses had access to investors and consultants, while small green entrepreneurs, who strive to do good for the environment, were left to fend for themselves. These entrepreneurs face a market driven by profit, where their environmentally friendly initiatives are often overlooked. This disparity fueled my desire to create something meaningful for them. I envisioned a platform that would support and uplift these "Green Voices." With Green Voice Networks (GVN), I aim to bridge this gap. Through GVN, I am committed to sharing my insights and strategies, gathered from extensive case studies, with these young entrepreneurs. My goal is to provide them with the resources, network, and support they need to thrive.
